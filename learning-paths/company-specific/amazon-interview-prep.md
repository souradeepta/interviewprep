## 🔑 Amazon-Specific Requirements

### Coding (40% of interview weight)

**What Amazon cares about:**
- Solving problem optimally
- Handling edge cases thoroughly
- Scalable thinking (what if N=1B?)

**Topics:**
1. **Arrays/Strings** (30%)
2. **Trees** (25%)
3. **Graphs** (20%)
4. **Hash maps** (10%)
5. **DP** (10%)
6. **Others** (5%)

### System Design (30%, for SDE2+)

**Focus:** Real Amazon systems
- E-commerce (inventory, orders)
- AWS services (S3, DynamoDB)
- Scaling concepts

### Behavioral (30%)

**Weighted heavily.** Leadership Principles evaluated through:
- STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Ownership examples
- Customer-centric thinking
- Diversity & inclusion

## 💼 Behavioral Interview Prep

### Structure: STAR Method

```
Situation: Where did this happen? When? Context?
Task: What was the challenge/goal?
Action: What did YOU do specifically?
Result: What was the outcome? Metrics preferred.
```

### Stories to Prepare (5-6)

1. **Ownership & Accountability**
   - Took on responsibility
   - Saw it through to completion

2. **Dealing with Conflict**
   - Disagreement with teammate
   - How you resolved it
   - Learning from it

3. **Customer Impact**
   - Understood customer need
   - Made decision based on that
   - Positive outcome

4. **Scaling/Thinking Big**
   - Took on large problem
   - Simplified or solved creatively

5. **Learning & Growth**
   - Made mistake
   - What you learned
   - How you changed behavior

6. **Technical Excellence**
   - High bar for quality
   - Pushed back on shortcuts
   - Positive result

## 🚀 Amazon-Specific Tips

### In the Interview

```
1. Ask about scale upfront
   "What's the data volume? 10 items or 1B?"
   Shows thinking big mindset

2. Discuss ownership
   "If I were owning this feature..."
   Demonstrates ownership principle

3. Explain trade-offs
   "We could do X (fast) or Y (more scalable)"
   Shows thinking about long-term

4. Consider customers
   "From customer perspective, they would want..."
   Shows customer obsession

5. Admit when stuck
   "I'm going to dive deeper here"
   Shows diving deep principle
```

### Behavioral Strategy

```
Each story should hit 2-3 leadership principles

Example: "Customer Obsession + Ownership"
- Heard customer complaint (Customer Obsession)
- Took responsibility to fix (Ownership)
- Implemented solution (Bias for Action)
- Result: Happy customer + team confidence
```

## ✅ Pre-Interview Checklist

**Coding:**
- [ ] Solved 60+ problems comfortably
- [ ] Can handle medium in 30-35 minutes
- [ ] Consider scalability (10x, 100x) naturally
- [ ] Handle edge cases thoroughly
- [ ] 3+ mock interviews done

**Behavioral:**
- [ ] Have 5-6 stories prepared (STAR format)
- [ ] Each story hits 2-3 leadership principles
- [ ] Stories are specific (names, outcomes, learnings)
- [ ] Can tell in 2-3 minutes naturally
- [ ] Rehearsed with friend/colleague

**System Design:**
- [ ] Understand database options
- [ ] Know caching strategies
- [ ] Can estimate capacity needs
- [ ] Familiar with Amazon services (S3, DynamoDB, etc.)
